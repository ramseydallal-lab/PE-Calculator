Simplified clinician-facing calculator guidance
Created: 30 Mar 2026 23:23:24
Use clinically entered concepts only: age, BMI, disseminated cancer, operative time, postoperative length of stay at discharge, ASA class, inpatient/outpatient status, and procedure selection.
For the update model, also record timing of first readmission and first reoperation within 30 days.
The bedside calculator omits baseline functional status to improve usability; the locked research manuscript model still includes that term.
Predicted risk reflects observed ACS-NSQIP outcomes under 2020-2024 care patterns and may be influenced by thromboprophylaxis or preventive care not fully captured in the source data.
Low predicted risk does not necessarily equal low untreated biologic thrombosis risk.
